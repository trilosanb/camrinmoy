The practice

A chartered accountant who builds systems, not just statements.

Mrinmoy & Co. is led by CA Mrinmoy Pathak — combining the discipline of the profession with a practical, technology-led way of working for businesses across Northeast India.

Biography

CA Mrinmoy Pathak

Mrinmoy is a Chartered Accountant and the principal of Mrinmoy & Co., with more than a decade of experience advising founders, MSMEs, institutions and government-linked bodies. His practice spans the full arc of a finance function — from book-keeping and assurance to tax, corporate compliance and board-level advisory.

What sets the work apart is a bias towards systems. Rather than treating compliance as a year-end scramble, he builds cloud-based accounting, dashboards and reporting that keep a business current all year — so decisions rest on numbers that are already reconciled and ready.

He completed the Leadership Development Programme at the Indian Institute of Management, Ahmedabad, which shapes how he approaches engagements: structured, outcome-focused, and grounded in how a business actually runs.

Mission

Make finance a source of clarity and confidence — not stress.

The mission is simple: give every client accounts they can trust, compliance they never have to worry about, and reporting clear enough to make real decisions from. Rigour and readability, together.

In practice

Traditional discipline, modern tools.

Accounting has always been about trust in the numbers. What has changed is how those numbers are produced and read. The practice sits at that intersection — pairing the profession's standards with cloud systems and dashboards, so a business owner in Guwahati has the same visibility as one anywhere in the world.
